Build the complete BLAB product flow using the SAME visual language, typography system, spacing, motion style, and editorial aesthetic as the landing page design.

Maintain:
- the premium editorial feel
- elegant serif typography
- cream/off-white backgrounds
- black typography
- muted salmon/deep red accents
- asymmetrical composition
- kinetic speech-inspired motion
- luxury minimal UI
- subtle waveform/speech visualization
- refined spacing and hierarchy

The app should feel like one continuous premium experience from landing page to product.

==================================================
PRODUCT FLOW
==================================================

1. SETUP SCREEN
Users select:
- language
- fluency level
- mode

Languages:
Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Mandarin, Arabic, Portuguese, Italian, Hindi

Levels:
- Beginner
- Intermediate
- Advanced

Modes:
- Normal
- Nightmare Mode

Nightmare Mode should feel psychologically intense and unpredictable.

Avoid standard dropdown UI.
Use elegant editorial selection components.

Primary CTA:
"FIND MY TOPIC"

==================================================
2. TOPIC GENERATION EXPERIENCE
==================================================

This is the signature cinematic moment.

The system should feel like:
an AI searching through thoughts, conversations, memories, and speech fragments.

Use:
- kinetic typography
- moving transcript fragments
- floating words
- subtle waveform motion
- dynamic text transitions
- intelligent scanning aesthetics

The system rapidly cycles through topic fragments before locking onto one final topic.

After selection:
- the final topic expands dramatically into focus
- everything else fades back
- show a 5-second countdown

Then recording starts automatically.

==================================================
3. RECORDING SCREEN
==================================================

The recording experience should feel:
focused, minimal, intense, performance-driven.

Include:
- live waveform visualization
- elegant recording timer
- subtle microphone indicator
- live speech transcript
- subtle real-time speaking analysis

Examples of live analysis:
- hesitation detected
- filler word spike
- confidence dropping
- long pause

Transcript should appear dynamically and elegantly, not like a plain textbox.

Primary CTA:
"STOP & SUBMIT"

==================================================
4. ANALYZING SCREEN
==================================================

Short transition state while AI analyzes speech.

Use:
- moving waveform lines
- transcript fragments
- subtle scanning motion
- elegant typography animations

Example analysis states:
- Analyzing fluency...
- Detecting hesitation patterns...
- Evaluating confidence...
- Measuring spontaneity...

The screen should feel intelligent and cinematic.

==================================================
5. FEEDBACK SCREEN
==================================================

This is the emotional payoff.

Display:
- large fluency score
- brutally honest coach line
- strengths
- weaknesses
- improved sentence suggestion
- XP earned

Structure:
- What You Did Well
- Where You Fell Apart
- The Sentence You Should Have Said

Feedback tone:
direct, psychologically sharp, brutally honest.

Examples:
- "You paused every time your ideas became complex."
- "Your confidence collapsed halfway through."
- "Too many filler words weakened your delivery."

Use elegant editorial layouts.
Avoid dashboard/card-heavy SaaS design.

==================================================
6. SHARE CARD GENERATOR
==================================================

Generate premium social-share cards matching the landing page aesthetic.

Include:
- topic
- score
- coach line
- rank
- BLAB branding

The card should feel:
minimal, cinematic, and post-worthy.

==================================================
7. RANK + XP SYSTEM
==================================================

Ranks:
- Mute
- Mumbler

- Ghost Speaker

Track:
- total XP
